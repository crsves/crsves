NOIR

NOIR — Multiplayer Typing Game

Real-time browser-based typing game in vanilla JS & Canvas API — no engine, no framework.

Destroy incoming enemy waves by typing their attached words. Two players share a synced enemy seed over WebSocket; first to type a word kills it for both. Watch your opponent's plane and shots in real time.

Canvas 2D · particle FX · screen shake · chromatic aberration · seeded PRNG · WebSocket relay · zero frontend deps
